Minneapolis vs St.Paul

The land of 10,000 lakes is more than a frozen permafrost!

It's a travel destination that invokes the envy of its neighbors, mostly from Wisconsin, who stare across the St. Croix, in awe of the greatness that is Minnesota. At the heart of the state are the powerhouse cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul, nestled along the banks of the mighty Mississippi River, within a stone's throw of each other. Sure, people come here for the lakes, the North Shore, and America's largest mall ... but if they're being honest, they'll tell you they're really here to sample our world-class culinary offerings - which prompt us to ask ...

 When it comes to food, which city does it better?
